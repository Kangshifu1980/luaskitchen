About Lua

From law firm to home kitchen

After 10 years working in a law firm, Lua Le decided to follow her true passion — sharing the food she grew up cooking. Originally from Northern Vietnam and having lived in Saigon for 27 years, she brings both sides of Vietnamese cuisine together in every class.

Lua first learned to cook at age five, learning traditional family recipes that she's been refining ever since. Her classes aren't just about food — they're about culture, conversation, and feeling at home in Vietnam.

"I would like to help travelers have the best, most unique experience and help them discover the city as much as possible."
